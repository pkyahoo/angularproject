about the haus

Koncept Haus is a creative design agency established in 2015 in Amman, Jordan. Complementing each other with their diverse backgrounds, the managing partners at Koncept Haus have gained vast experience in event management, interiors, lighting, and furniture design amongst other fields.

Koncept Haus specializes in concept development and event management for corporate, weddings and social functions.

Operating both locally and internationally, The Koncept Haus aims to transform your wildest concepts into reality.
